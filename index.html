About PAMS

PAMS (Patient Appointment Management System) is a user-friendly platform designed to streamline the appointment scheduling process for patients and healthcare providers. Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for both parties, allowing patients to easily book appointments online and healthcare providers to efficiently manage their schedules.
